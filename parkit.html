Overview

In a densely populated country like India, it is difficult to find a parking spot. Due to scarcity of parking spaces, people tend to park vehicles on road side which causes traffic problems. To avoid this problem, a digital solution can be provided which can help users book parking slots online in a hassle free manner and rest assured that they have a parking space available for them in advance. This will not only help the parking space owners to manage their parking spaces but also encourage more people to lend spaces for parking to make some extra money. The application ParkIt allows users to book parking slots in advance using a simple and easy to comprehend interface.

Logo Design

The app's name is ParkIt, and it encourages users to book parking spaces through it. Because we're generally dealing with car parking spaces, the logo includes a car symbol. The logo is designed by blending the letter P with the car symbol. The shade of blue evokes a sense of relaxation and weightlessness. This cool tranquil blue promotes calm and trust, as well as patience. Blue is frequently associated with stability and dependability. When it comes to parking spaces in India, one must be patient and deal with the chaotic situation.
