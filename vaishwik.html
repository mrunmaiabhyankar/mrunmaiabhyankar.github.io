Overview

With the advancement of technology, everyone is shifting to digitization. Virtually visiting an art gallery is something that has never been done before, but it is doable. Vaishwik is a small art gallery that displays many beautiful works of art but is unable to reach a larger audience. A tablet app will help us broaden their reach and connect art enthusiasts to some stunning paintings. The app's basic functionality allows users to browse art works, learn more about them, and purchase the ones they like. The app makes it simple to buy and sell paintings without having to physically visit the gallery every time.

Conclusion

Vaishwik is a small art gallery in Pune that wants to create a tablet app to display its stunning works of art digitally and enable remote customers to view their art in order to expand their reach. This was made possible by the creation of an interactive tablet application with basic features to boost sales of Vaishwik Art Gallery's artwork. Additional features such as online auctions, android and iOS integration, artist profiles, and more could be added later on. If designed on a large scale, a small software could assist in resolving the issues of numerous such art galleries.
