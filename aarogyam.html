Overview

In Sanskrit, the word 'Aarogyam' signifies "health." Sanskrit is regarded as the mother tongue of all languages. As a result, it is an extremely relatable language. Because Indian holy writings are penned in Sanskrit, the word 'Aarogyam' also conveys a feeling of protection. The application allows users to maintain their digital wellness by monitoring their activity, spending quality time on self development and using psychologically proven calming methods to maintain a healthy balance in the world overpowered by technology.
